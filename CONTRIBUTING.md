Thank you for your interest in contributing to the Rhandum Townhall design archive.

This project welcomes community insight, discussion, and creative sparks while preserving clear authorship boundaries.


Contributions fall into two categories:

1. Discussion-Based Contributions
The Discussion Boards are the core of community interaction. Anyone may post:

-Ideas

-Balance suggestions

-Lore concepts

-Humor, thematic dialogue

-Feedback on existing proposals

-General conversation in the Community Gathering

These discussions help guide the direction of future design work.

Community members whose ideas inspire a mechanic, theme, or structural change will be credited in:
Rhandum_Townhall / Hall_of_Fame

This includes:

-Inspiration sources

-Commenters who influenced concepts

-Memorable community contributions

-Notable participation, humor, or support

Being listed does not transfer authorship or copyright; it recognizes influence and involvement.

2. Formal Design Suggestions

If you want to your idea considered for integration into a future batch:
Submit it in the Poet_of_Inspirations discussions.

If you have balancing or mechanic critiques, submit in the Balance_Council discussion

If you have ideas for the Rhandum lore or personalities of units and heroes submit in the Balance_Council discussion

Please provide:
-A clear description of the mechanic, critique or lore idea

-The intended gameplay impact

-Any constraints, risks, or synergy notes

Your idea may be:
-Accepted and adapted

-Reworked

-Rejected (with explanation whenever possible)


I READ EVERY COMMENT!!

Again, Ideas incorporated into the final design notes will be credited in the Hall_of_Fame.
How Final Integration Works
To protect clarity and maintain a unified design vision:
All merged designs are authored, edited, and finalized by the project creator.


Community ideas may inspire or shape mechanics, but the final structure remains curated.


Rights to the final compiled designs remain with the project creator under the repository license.


What Not to Submit
Direct copyrighted text, art, or assets from MY.GAMES


Reproductions of in-game files


Requests to copy monetized features


Personal attacks or low-effort spam


Attempts to circumvent licensing
