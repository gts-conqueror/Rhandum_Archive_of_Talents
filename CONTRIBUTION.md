# Rhandum Townhall - Code of Conduct

To keep this archive welcoming, structured, and creatively focused, all participants are expected to follow these guidelines.

---

## 1. Respectful Interaction

- Debate mechanics, not people.
- No insults, harassment, discrimination, or targeting of individuals or groups.
- Treat all community members and any MY.GAMES staff who may view this project with respect and professionalism.

---

## 2. Constructive Contributions

- Critique mechanics only when your contribution adds value.
- Offer feedback with clarity and reasoning, not hostility.
- When suggesting changes, explain *why* they improve balance, clarity, or identity.
- Avoid spam, repetitive posts, or low-effort comments that disrupt discussion flow.

---

## 3. No Intellectual Property Violations

- Do **not** post copyrighted material from MY.GAMES or any other publisher.
- Do **not** copy or upload in-game text, icons, art, files, or protected assets.
- High-level references, summaries, and discussions of existing units are allowed.
- Direct asset reproduction is not.

---

## 4. No False Representation

- Do not claim to be a developer, MY.GAMES employee, or official representative.
- Do not impersonate other community members.
- This project is an independent, community-facing archive.

---

## 5. Safe Environment for Minors

- No explicit, graphic, or inappropriate content.
- No excessive violence, sexual material, or offensive language.
- This project is intended to remain suitable for younger players and general audiences.

---

## 6. Maintain Creative Integrity

- Ideas shared in discussions remain owned by their original authors.
- Final curated designs within the repository are authored and maintained by the project owner.
- Contributors will be credited when their ideas inspire final features.
- Credit does **not** imply authorship transfer or ownership of compiled works.

---

## 7. Reporting Issues

If you encounter a violation of this Code of Conduct:

- Flag the post within GitHub Discussions  
  **or**
- Contact the repository owner directly

Violations may result in:
- Comment removal
- Temporary restrictions
- Permanent removal from discussions, depending on severity

---

## Commitment

By participating in this project, you agree to uphold the spirit of collaboration, creativity, and respect that flows through the halls of the Townhall.
