# Chemist

## Unit Overview

**Role:** Debuff **|**
**Rarity:** Rare **|**
**Core Mechanic:** Destroys enemy armour, increasing damage taken from all sources. **|**

### Current State
Chemist excels at armour destruction in the late waves where stacked armour reduction compounds across a full board  in the right composition it quietly enables enormous DPS from allied units. Outside of that supporting role, Chemist has no unique identity: it doesn't attack in a meaningful way, lacks a strong incentive goal and brings nothing to early waves where armour destruction has less impact. The unit is a background variable rather than a unit worth building around.

### Design Goal
These talents introduce the Principal Investigator and Lab Assistant variants to give Chemist an active, visible research identity with new forms of board interaction slowing, boosting, and structuring play through a chemistry metaphor that feels distinct from every other unit in the game. A player running Chemist well should feel like they are managing a lab: scheduling experiments, distributing reagents at the right moment, and watching the compounding effects of good preparation pay off mid-wave.

---

## Talentry Proposal
The Talents of Chemist Introduce new variants called:
- **Principal Investigator** (*variant*)
- **Lab Assistant** (*variant*)

---

### 1st Talent

- **Left: Enhance Reagent Prototype v1 (Red)**
  - The highest merge rank Chemist becomes the "Principal Investigator."
  - The Principal Investigator stops attacking for 17s to conduct an experiment.
  - After the experiment: Distributes a new potion to other Chemists, boosting armor destruction by +5% for each Chemist's current value (15s duration).
  - This process repeats.

- **Right: Enhanced Reagent Prototype v2 (Blue)**
  - The highest merge rank Chemist becomes the "Principal Investigator."
  - The Principal Investigator stops attacking for 17s to conduct an experiment.
  - After the experiment: Distributes a new potion to other Chemists, who gain the ability to slow monsters by 5% (15s duration).
  - This process repeats.

---

### 2nd Talent

- **Left: High Concentration**
  - Principal Investigator’s armor destruction/slowing effect is boosted by an additional 2%.

- **Right: Conjugate Haste**
  - Principal Investigator's brewing time is reduced to 15s.

---

### 3rd Talent

- **Left: Octet Rule**
  - When the total combined merge rank of Chemists is a multiple of 8:  
    - All units adjacent to a Chemist(s) increase critical chance by 1% and attack speed by 2% per merge rank of the adjacent chemist.
      
    *SFX / VFX to indicate reached threshold.*
    
    *Stack counter to show total combined mergerank.*

- **Right: Redox Reaction**
  - Dragging a Principal Investigator onto a unit of the same merge rank or lower puts set unit in a "reduced" state, granting +3% crit chance for that single unit and increasing the specific unit's attack speed by 5% for 20s.
  - Afterward, the Principal Investigator becomes "oxidized," disabling the production of Reagents for 15s after the reaction.
   
   *SFX / VFX to indicate Principal Investigator re-activation.*
---

### Final Talent

- **De-localized Irrigation**  
  Introduces the "Lab Assistant," who sets up 4 hanging bars like irrigation apparatuses on the path.
  - Bars become active for 15s, then cooldown for 30s.
  - Can only secrete a reagent prototype.
  - Effects from the irrigation system are enhanced by 40%.
  - *(Each bar takes 1 wave to set up. The Lab Assistant must remain on board until setup is complete, else progress is lost.)*

  *SFX / VFX to indicate setup and re-activation.*
