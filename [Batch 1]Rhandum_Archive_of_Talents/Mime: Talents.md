# Mime

## Unit Overview

**Role:** Special **|**
**Rarity:** Epic **|**
**Core Mechanic:** Merges with any unit type, functioning as a quality-of-life merge facilitator.

### Current State
Mime works best alongside units whose abilities trigger on merge, it accelerates activation reliably and fills gaps in board composition with flexibility. That flexibility is also its only 'trick'. Mime has no independent combat contribution, making it a single-purpose tool that becomes deadweight once its merge utility has been used or is no longer needed. In a meta that demands consistent output from every board slot, Mime struggles to justify its place outside niche deck requirements

### Design Goal
These talents give Mime an active on-board presence beyond its merge utility, introducing mask mechanics and performance-based effects that contribute independently of whether a merge is available. A player running Mime well should feel like they are managing a performance: timing mask throws, reading the board for rebound chains, and turning what was once a passive facilitator into a dynamic support unit.

## Talentry Proposal
This proposal transitions the Mime from a simple merge-fodder unit into the Mask Maker, a dynamic support specialist that utilizes "Rebound" mechanics to circulate powerful buffs and disruption

## 1st Talent

- **Left: Eiffel Joy**
  - Introduces the "Mask Maker" type Mime.
  - Every 30s, creates a Joy mask:
    - Boosts attack speed by 10%.
    - Thrown to a random unit for 15s.
    - If it lands on a Mime, effect is boosted by 1% and rebounds to another random unit. Max boost: 5%.

- **Right: Eiffel Sorrow**
  - Introduces "Mask Maker" type Mime.
  - Every 30s, creates a Rage mask:
    - Grants 5% armor destruction.
    - Thrown to a random unit for 15s.
    - If it lands on a Mime, effect boosted by 0.5% and rebounds. Max boost: 5%.

 >- *Pronounced “I feel Joy/Sorrow”*

## 2nd Talent

- **Left: Exaggeration**
  - Mask effects have 10% chance to last 25% longer.

- **Right: Productive Silence**
  - Mask Maker has a 10% chance to throw 2 masks.

---

## 3rd Talent

- **Left: Seeing is Believing**
  - After 6 acts (merges), Mask Maker manifests an invisible barrier, stunning monster movement for 4s.

- **Right: Deafening Visuals**
  - After 6 acts (merges), summons monsters to the opponent’s board that exert the negative characteristic of the selected 1st talent onto their units.
