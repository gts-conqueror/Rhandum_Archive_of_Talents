# Mime

## Base Mechanic Adjustment
- Gains a mana ability reducing the merge rank of the highest-rank Mime (cannot go below 1).
- Cooldown: **TBD by developers**
- *Pronounced “I feel Joy/Sorrow”*

---

## 1st Talent

- **Left: Eiffel Joy**
  - Introduces the "Mask Maker" type Mime.
  - Every 30s, creates a Joy mask:
    - Boosts attack speed by 10%.
    - Thrown to a random unit for 15s.
    - If it lands on a Mime, effect is boosted by 1% and rebounds to another random unit. Max boost: 5%.

- **Right: Eiffel Sorrow**
  - Introduces "Mask Maker" type Mime.
  - Every 30s, creates a Rage mask:
    - Grants 5% armor destruction.
    - Thrown to a random unit for 15s.
    - If it lands on a Mime, effect boosted by 0.5% and rebounds. Max boost: 5%.
 
 
 *Visual & Sound cues indicate rebound.*

---

## 2nd Talent

- **Left: Exaggeration**
  - Mask effects have 10% chance to last 25% longer.

- **Right: Productive Silence**
  - Mask Maker has a 10% chance to throw 2 masks.

---

## 3rd Talent

- **Left: Seeing is Believing**
  - After 6 acts (merges), Mask Maker manifests an invisible barrier, stunning monster movement for 4s.

- **Right: Deafening Visuals**
  - After 6 acts (merges), summons monsters to the opponent’s board that exert the negative characteristic of the selected talent onto their units.

---

## Final Talent

- **Final Performance / Marceau's Performance**
  - Mask Maker entertains the first boss, delaying its ability activation by 50%.
   
   *Unique animation of the mime that shows this effect*
